In scope of Udacity course [Intro to iOS App Development with Swift][udacity] I build my first iOS App on Swift language.

You can record your voice and add 4 effects:
- Chipmunk (Pitch up)
- Darth Vader (Pitch down)
- Rabbit (Speed up)
- Snail (Speed down)
